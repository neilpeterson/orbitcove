# Privacy Policy

## OrbitCove Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

### Our Commitment

OrbitCove is committed to protecting your privacy. This document outlines our privacy practices and your rights regarding your personal data.

### Data Collection

**What We Collect:**
- Community names and descriptions (created by you)
- User profile information (name, email)
- Community membership information

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Your messages or communications content
- Your browsing behavior
- Your location data
- Any analytics or tracking data

### Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:
- Provide the OrbitCove service
- Enable community features
- Improve user experience

We NEVER:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your data without your consent
- Mine your data for any purpose

### Data Storage

- **Local-First**: All data is stored locally on your device by default
- **Encryption**: All communications use end-to-end encryption
- **Control**: You can export or delete your data at any time

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct your data
- Delete your data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of any data collection

### Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- End-to-end encryption for all communications
- Secure data storage
- Regular security audits
- No third-party tracking

### Children's Privacy

OrbitCove is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

### Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the app.

### Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us through the GitHub repository.
